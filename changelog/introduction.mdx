## Apr 20, 2026

### Feature Updates

**Edit Extra Cost and Savings library items from Formats page**

You can now edit Extra Costs and Savings from the Formats. Previously, you had to go to the Settings page to edit Extra Costs and Savings in the library. 

<Note>
  Be careful when updating the library from the Formats page. This will affect any Formats where this Extra Cost or Saving is used.
</Note>

**Double-Click to View Records**

You can now set your useability settings to display records when double-clicking the row in a table. Previously, you had to press the row and then select "View" from the preview menu. 

To turn this feature on, switch on "Enable double-click to open detail view" from your Profile settings.
